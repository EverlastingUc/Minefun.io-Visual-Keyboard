## Version 1.0.0

- **Release Date:** 29 June 2026
- **Author:** Everlasting
- **Contributors:** None

### What's New

- Full keyboard overlay with all standard keys.
- Real-time key press detection and highlighting.
- 6 animation effects: lift, bounce, rotate, pulse, shake, wobble.
- Gradient support for keys.
- Panel customization: color, gradient, background image.
- Glow and shadow effects.
- Drag to reposition keyboard anywhere on screen.
- Settings panel with live preview.
- Auto-save settings to localStorage.
- Keyboard shortcut: Alt+Ctrl+K to toggle overlay.
- Light and dark mode support.
- Help section for new users.
